First off, here is a small walkthrough for the campaign missions. They are really easy on medium but there are still some tips to save a lot of time.

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

After the campaign, there are a bunch of gun varients that you need to know.

 

Assault

SHOTGUNS:

factory: less recoil, faster accuracy recovery

slug: one big bullet

hunter: more range, tighter spray

sweeper: more spray

backbored: more recoil recovery, faster damage dropoff

extended: bigger mag

SMGS:

storm: less recoil

trench: better hip fire

factory: less recoil

optical: better accuracy

experimental: accurate burst fires

 

MEDIC:

factory: less recoil

trench: better hip fire

optical: better accuracy

marksmen: better accuracy when standing still (scope)

sweeper: fully automatic mode, better hip fire

storm: reduced recoil

sniper: better accuracy when standing still (has bipod)

extended: larger mag, less recoil, less damage

 

Support:

factory: less recoil

trench: better hip fire

optical: better accuracy

low weight: same as factory

suppressive: large mag, bipod

telescopic: better accuracy, bipod

 

Scout:

Sniper: higher magnification scope and bipod

marksmen: medium magnification scope and longer breath hold, No scope glint when zooming in

trench: weaker bullet that can be fired faster

experimental: same as trench

infantry: less recoil, gains accuracy quicker
